Meet Michael Maraia

A life turned into music.

Michael Maraia is an Army veteran, singer, songwriter, storyteller, and lifelong country music lover behind MyZ Backroads. His music is rooted in lived experience—survival, recovery, faith, loneliness, courage, and the choice to keep going.

My Country Story

The songs became hope.

I've been singing since I was 12 years old.

After surviving a suicide attempt, I began living in a children's home in Florida. There, a new friend introduced me to country music. Those songs became more than music—they became hope.

My first country concert featured Kenny Rogers and Tammy Wynette in the early 1980s. That night changed my life.

Not long afterward, I saw Dolly Parton in concert. I still remember her beautiful stage that looked like a country field. I've loved everything about country life ever since.

Today I continue writing songs inspired by faith, veterans, recovery, horses, and the country way of life.

I'm currently working on a new song called “Country Life.”
